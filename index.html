Sports.

Tansa House has several Sports Facilities and Equipments like Volley-ball, table-soccer, cricket, badminton, table-tennis etc. It also has gym Facilities within the hostel premises.

Library
Facilities

Library in the Tansa House is well air-conditioned and provides peaceful enviornment for students to study. It has well furnished study table and chairs, books for all the genres for students having different goals.

About Hostel

Tansa House is a residential premises present within the IIT Bombay campus. Tansa is a rich blend of intellects coming from various parts of India and thus celebration mode persists round the year. The residents herein are popularly known as 'Tansaite'. Tansaites, are the core people who are postgraduate students of sciences at IIT Bombay. Tansaites are involved in each type event organised by institute and various councils of institute.
